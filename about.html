Prophetic Flames of Fire Ministries — Healing Nations Church

Our Vision & Mission

Prophetic Flames of Fire Ministries (Healing Nations Church) exists to bring God's healing power to individuals, families, and communities across Zambia and beyond.

Our Mission: To release the prophetic voice, demonstrate the love of Christ, and see souls transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision: Equipping people through the prophetic voice — raising a generation that carries healing, deliverance, and the sound of heaven to the nations.
