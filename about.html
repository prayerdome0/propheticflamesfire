About Our Ministry

Prophetic Flames of Fire Ministries (Healing Nations Church) exists to bring God's healing power to individuals, families, and communities across Zambia and beyond. Through prayer, the Word, and compassionate service, we believe every nation can experience restoration.

Our Mission: To release the prophetic voice, demonstrate the love of Christ, and see souls transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit. We are a people of worship, intercession, and radical faith.

What We Believe: We believe in the Triune God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. The Bible is the infallible Word of God. Salvation is through Jesus Christ alone. We believe in the gifts of the Spirit, healing, prophecy, deliverance, and the soon return of our King.

  • Faith in Jesus Christ for salvation
  • The power of the prophetic voice for our generation
  • Divine healing and deliverance ministry
  • The Great Commission to all nations
